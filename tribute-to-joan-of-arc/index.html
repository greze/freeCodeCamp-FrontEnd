Joan of Arc

There's a lot of people in this band

Joan of Arc is an experimental music group from Chicago. While the band features many members, Tim Kinsella is the only consistent and constant member. The band is known for their work with electronics, samples and multi-track songs.

"The only thing we've in common is that nobody can tell the difference between us"
